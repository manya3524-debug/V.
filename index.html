Things I Never Said

This is not an apology paragraph.

This is just a small archive of things I should have said better.

Proceed carefully. Penguin emotions ahead.

1. What I Got Wrong

I made things heavier than they needed to be.

I reacted badly when I should have understood better.

And sometimes, instead of making you feel safe, I made you feel tired.

2. What I Learned

I learned that effort is not just saying sorry.

It is listening properly.

It is not repeating the same thing again.

It is making someone feel peaceful, not responsible for your emotions.

3. What I Admire About You

You stayed in moments where most people would have left.

You listened to things you never signed up for.

You became important in the most unexpected and inconvenient way.

4. What I’ll Carry Forward

The calls.

The stupid jokes.

The “God’s plan” trauma.

The way even your annoying lines became familiar.

🐧

5. Thank You

You don’t have to reply to this.

You don’t have to forgive anything because of this.

I just wanted you to know that you mattered to me.

A lot.

Okay bye, the penguin is pretending to be normal now.
